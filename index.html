About this

Hello, my name is Jay Bhatt and I am a recent college graduate with a passion for learning and adventures. My favorite topics to learn about include: money, food, travel, mechanical engineering, and computer science. This blog is a personal project of mine to help me get better at coding (HTML & CSS), writing and cooking. Since this project, combines ⅘ of my favorite topics, hope you have as much fun reading it as I will writing it!

Who is this blog for?

People who are interested in getting to understand how to tackle life while maintaining some sort of wealth, health and most importantly themselves.

This blog is a work in progress and as I learn to add additional features to this project, the better it should get. My next big step is to add a comments section so be on the lookout for that. Currently, I don’t know how to sign people up for a newsletter so for now if you want to just bookmark this page, that’d be great :)

Thank you for reading this and if at any stage of your visit, a wall of writing seems like too much, just look at the pretty pictures. I am working on making the articles shorter! Currently, I have the most amount of content in travel but I am also working on adding more things to food and money!
