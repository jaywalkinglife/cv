About Travel

As Harun Yahya once said, “I always wonder why birds choose to stay in the same place when they can fly anywhere on the earth, then I ask myself the same question”. I asked myself this very question my sophomore year of college. The answer was simple: I didn’t have anyone to go with. To find a solution, I utilized social media, and reached out to some distant friends and soon I was headed to Panama City Beach for my first ever “solo” trip. My second trip was a backpacking trip through Europe and, I was hooked.

I realized traveling doesn’t have to be expensive, all you really need are airplane tickets (preferably on sale), friends (preferably, good ones), a camera, food and hostels.

  • Mission: Travel to every major country on this beautiful Earth.
  • Current Goal: Travel to all seven continents!
