Cooking ahead of time saves money, builds confidence and makes you healthier and more concious about your day to day life. So practice this sweet magic! Below are some awesome recipes to get you started!

General Tips

  1. Clean everything up as you cook, you have the momentum, use it! - p.s Dishwashers are for the weak
  2. Prep ahead, if you have everything (ingredients) ready to be sacrificed to the flames, the cooking will be a breeze
  3. Listen to some music, I know everyone says, "Trust the process", I say "Enjoy the process"

  • Egg Bites

    These things are simple and packed with protein, I recommend eating two at a time which will give you more than enough motivation to avoid those unhealthy bagels and hashbrowns and hold you off till a nice healthy lunch! If you are rich then you can substitute whole eggs with egg whites to eliminate the insane amount of cholesterol you consume. However, if you are active and want to save money, you can just eat the whole egg.

    What you need:

    1. 13 large eggs
    2. 2 ounces of veggies like spinach, broccoli, kale, etc. (optional but it will make this recipe healthier!)
    3. 4 slices of muenster cheese (feel free to substitute with your favorite cheese)
    4. 2 tbsp Olive oil (feel free to substitute with butter or coconut oil)

    What to do:

    • Step 1: Preheat the over to 400 degrees F
    • Step 2: Use some sort of oil (olive oil, cocunut oil, etc.) and spray it over the muffin tray so that your egg bites are easy to remove
    • Step 3: Crack 13 eggs and place them in a giant container, if you don't have a big enough container, then crack them in batches
    • Step 4: Whip those 13 eggs into a nice yellow fluid
    • Step 5: Pour the liquid in the muffin cups and add some sort of seasoning (pepper, garlic powder, garlic & herbs, etc.)
    • Step 6: Add some vegetables in the liquid (skip if you hate vegges, but really you should definitely eat some veggies, they are good for you, David!)
    • Step 7: Add some delicious cheese on top! I typically take a slice of cheese and split into three and place 1/3 of the slice in each muffin cup.
    • Step 8: Put the tray in the oven and set the timer to 17-19 minutes (So, 18 minutes)
    • Step 9: Wash dishes while the eggbites cook!
    • Step 10: After washing dishes, go run a mile
    • Step 11: After you have physically exerted yourself and its been 17-19 minutes, pull out the egg bites from the oven and let it cool for about 10-20 minutes
    • Step 12: Once cool, please them in tupperware and eat two at a time, which means each batch should last you more than a week!

    Heating instruction: Heat 2 eggbites in a standard microwave for around 1:00-1:30 minutes at work. If you have access to a mini-over, these will taste even better!

    What you need:

    1. A standard shaker cup
    2. 8-12 ounces of fat-free milk (feel free to substitute with other types of milk)
    3. 34 grams of standard Oats (utilize a protein powder scoop for measurement)
    4. 1 scoop of your favorite protein powder.
    5. 2 tbsp of chia seeds

    What to do:

    • Step 1: Pour everything in a shaker cup, shake it and put it in the fridge.
    • Step 2: Run a mile, take a shower, and go to sleep.
    • Step 3: When leaving for work or school, grab Shaker cup and consume the sweet energy filled on-the-go breakfast.
